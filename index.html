Methodology

We extract complaint text from SEC litigation releases and predict 7 outcome metrics: resolution type (settled vs litigated), disgorgement amount, civil penalty, prejudgment interest, injunction, officer/director bar, and conduct restrictions.

Ground truth is extracted from SEC release text after cases resolve. Models see only the original complaint—no outcome information. Monetary predictions use 10% tolerance; categorical use exact match.
