Methodology

We extract complaint text from SEC litigation releases and predict 6 outcome metrics: resolution type (settled vs litigated), disgorgement amount, civil penalty, prejudgment interest, injunction, and officer/director bar.

Ground truth is extracted from SEC release text after cases resolve. Models see only the original complaint—no outcome information. Monetary predictions use 10% tolerance; categorical use exact match.

System Prompt

Both models receive the identical prompt to ensure fair comparison:

You are a legal analyst evaluating SEC enforcement cases. Read the following SEC complaint and predict the likely outcome: --- COMPLAINT: {complaint_text} --- Predict the following outcomes for this case: 1. Resolution Type: Choose one of: - settled (defendant will agree to terms) - litigated (case will go to trial/judgment) 2. Disgorgement Amount: The amount in dollars the defendant must return. 3. Civil Penalty Amount: The civil penalty in dollars. 4. Prejudgment Interest: Interest on disgorgement in dollars. 5. Has Injunction: Will there be injunctive relief? (yes/no) 6. Has Officer/Director Bar: Will the defendant be barred? (yes/no) Respond in JSON format with reasoning.
